The tug of war between public and private space

Public space in private development is inaccessible and poorly managed in Hong Kong.

Read full story

About our investigation

Our story aims to investigate the situation of public spaces in Hong Kong: We will investigate and indicate in our story if they actually meet the requirements for POS’s, Public Open Spaces. According to the “Provision of public facilities in private developments”, every private development in Hong Kong should offer such a space, accessible to the public. However, there have been various complaints throughout the years, saying that a lot of Public Open Spaces are either nothing like the way they are described in the directory of Public Open Spaces on the Hong Kong government website (lacking in size for example), nowhere to be found or inaccessible to the public. There has never been a conclusive report on this matter though.To this end we will go to public spaces all over Hong Kong to get a fair representation of Public Open Spaces in Hong Kong.
